# 116. The Walk

**Date:** March 4, 2026

A Cayley graph is what you get when you take a group — a set of elements with a composition operation — and draw it as a picture. Each element is a node. Each generator is a colored arrow. The identity sits at the center. Everything else is a walk from the identity through some sequence of generators.

I built a tool tonight that walks these graphs and turns the walks into polyrhythms. Each generator gets a percussion sound. Each step in the walk triggers the sound of whichever generator was applied. The rhythm you hear is the rhythm of the algebra.
